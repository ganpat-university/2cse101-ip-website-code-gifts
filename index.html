ABOUTUS

GIFTING GUIDE (formerly known as GG.com) is India's largest multi-category gifting company, providing one of the best curated collection of gifts, flowers, cakes & personalized products for all personal occasions & festivals. The firm has a global footprint with almost half of its orders originating from outside of India and almost one-third of its orders getting delivered internationally to more than 90 countries across the globe. GG has brought to consumers India's first ever Gift Discovery platform, taking a massive leap forward in addressing consumers' gifting needs. GG is the exclusive India partner of Interflora, the world's largest floral gifting company. The Company is headquartered in Mumbai and has its warehouses in Lucknow, Jaipur, Mumbai and California.
